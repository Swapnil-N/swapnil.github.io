## Barcelona

Gaudí's Sagrada Familia, wandering through the Gothic Quarter, and La Boqueria market. The beach at Barceloneta was the perfect way to end each day.

## Madrid

The Prado, Retiro Park, and some of the best tapas I've ever had. Madrid's nightlife is legendary — dinner at 10pm feels early.
