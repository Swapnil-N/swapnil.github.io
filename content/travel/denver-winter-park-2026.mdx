## Denver

Back in Denver again — at this point it feels like a second home. Quick stop for a night to grab dinner in LoDo, hit up a brewery, and get a good night's sleep before heading to the mountains. Denver in March still has that crisp mountain air, and the anticipation of a ski trip makes everything better.

## Winter Park

Winter Park is a locals' mountain with serious terrain. The Mary Jane side has some of the best bumps in Colorado, and we spent most of our time there getting our legs burned. The back side of the mountain is wide open and perfect for high-speed cruising when you need a break from the moguls.

The town of Winter Park is small but has everything you need — good restaurants, a couple of solid bars, and the kind of laid-back vibe you want after a day on the hill. Late March meant spring conditions — warm afternoons, soft snow, and longer days on the mountain. Another successful Colorado ski trip in the books, and I'm already eyeing the next one.
