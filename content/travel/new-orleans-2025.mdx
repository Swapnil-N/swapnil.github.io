## New Orleans

New Orleans is one of those cities that just hits different. From the moment you step off the plane, there's this energy — a mix of music, food, and a general attitude that life is meant to be enjoyed. We packed a lot into a long weekend and somehow still felt like we barely scratched the surface.

Bourbon Street at night is pure chaos in the most entertaining way possible, but the real magic is in the side streets — Frenchmen Street for live jazz, the Garden District for those jaw-dropping historic homes, and basically any random corner where someone's playing a trumpet. The food was on another level: beignets at Cafe Du Monde (obviously), gumbo that made me question every bowl I'd had before, and a po'boy that I still think about.

We took a streetcar through the Garden District, wandered through the French Quarter until our feet gave out, and closed each night with live music and one too many hurricanes. New Orleans doesn't let you leave without making an impression.
