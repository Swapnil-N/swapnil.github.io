## Tokyo

The city hit me like a wall of sound and light. Shibuya crossing at rush hour is sensory overload in the best way possible.

## Hakone

After the intensity of Tokyo, Hakone offered hot springs and mountain views. The cable car ride over Owakudani was surreal.

## Kyoto

Ancient temples, bamboo groves, and the most peaceful gardens I've ever seen. Fushimi Inari at dawn, with no crowds, was the highlight of the trip.
