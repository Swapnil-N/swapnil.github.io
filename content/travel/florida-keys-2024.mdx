## Miami

Starting in Miami before heading south — South Beach, Little Havana, and the best Cuban coffee.

## The Overseas Highway

One of the most scenic drives in America. 113 miles of bridges and islands connecting the mainland to Key West.

## Key West

The southernmost point in the continental US. Duval Street, snorkeling, and watching the sunset at Mallory Square.
