## Costa Rica

Costa Rica is the kind of place that reminds you why you travel. From the moment we arrived, the "pura vida" philosophy was everywhere — not just a saying, but a genuine way of life. The biodiversity here is staggering. We saw toucans, howler monkeys, sloths, and more species of frogs than I knew existed, all within the first couple of days.

We split our time between the rainforest and the coast. The cloud forests were otherworldly — hanging bridges through the canopy, zip lines over the treetops, and hiking trails where every turn revealed something new. The sounds of the jungle at night are a whole experience on their own.

The beaches on the Pacific side were perfect for unwinding. Warm water, consistent surf, and sunsets that painted the sky in impossible colors. We tried surfing (with mixed results), ate fresh ceviche on the sand, and generally embraced the slow pace. Costa Rica does nature and adventure better than almost anywhere, and the whole trip felt like a reset button for the soul.
