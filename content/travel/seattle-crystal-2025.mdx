## Seattle

Seattle in the spring is gorgeous — everything is green, the air is fresh, and if you're lucky, Rainier is out in full glory. We spent a day exploring the city before heading to the mountain. Pike Place Market was a must, obviously — fresh seafood, flowers, and the original Starbucks for the photo op. Capitol Hill had great coffee and even better people-watching. The food scene is legit — incredible pho, amazing sushi, and craft beer on every corner.

## Crystal Mountain

Crystal Mountain in late April is spring skiing at its finest. The snow was soft, the sun was out, and the views of Mount Rainier from the summit are absolutely unreal — it feels like you could reach out and touch it. We lapped the mountain all day, skiing in t-shirts by the afternoon. There's something special about spring skiing — the vibe is relaxed, the runs are empty, and the sundeck beers hit different when you're staring at a 14,000-foot volcano. A perfect little getaway.
